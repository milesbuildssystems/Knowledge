Your website and Google page
don't mean you'll get chosen.
A website only works if the right people see it. A Google page only works if it makes someone trust you enough to call. Most notaries set them up once, leave them sitting there, and wonder why the phone still isn't ringing.

Business cards and flyers
don't create follow-up.
Handing someone your card might make them aware of you. But awareness is not the same as demand. If there's no reason to remember you, no follow-up, and no next step, you're just hoping they call later.
Giving people your information is not a client system.
